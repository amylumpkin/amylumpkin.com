Get To Know Me!

Hi, I'm Amy! I'm a native of Raleigh, NC and still call the area home. I love being close to lots of hiking trails, cool restaurants downtown, and we're only a couple of hours away from the mountains and the beach! I have three rescue dogs who rule my world; Lydia, Henry and Clyde.

I'm also a web developer with knowledge of the full stack. I am seeking to utilize my diverse experiences and skill sets, as well as my natural aptitude for being a detail oriented and caring individual to excel in a role with a great company. Versed in HTML, CSS, Bootstrap, JavaScript, jQuery, Node.js, Express, SQL, MongoDB, React, sorting algorithms, testing and more!. Graduated from UNC's full-stack coding bootcamp in May 2018.
